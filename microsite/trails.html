Cedar Falls Trail

The park's most iconic hike descends into a cedar canyon to reach the 95-foot waterfall — Arkansas' most photographed. The trail out and back passes massive boulders and stunning cliff walls.

Before You Hike

  • Bring at least 1 liter of water per person per hour of hiking in warm weather.
  • Trails can be slippery after rain — waterproof boots are recommended on Canyon Trail.
  • Cell service is limited on the mountain. Download offline maps before you go.
  • All pets must be on a leash 6 feet or shorter at all times on all trails.
  • Check the weather forecast before setting out — afternoon thunderstorms are common in summer.
  • Leave No Trace: pack out all trash. Collecting rocks, plants, or artifacts is prohibited.
